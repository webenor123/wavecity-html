Welcome

Sri Sri Radha Mohan Madan Temple

ISKCON Wavecity Temple, also known as Sri Sri Radha Madan Mohan Mandir, is a peaceful place dedicated to Vedic learning & spiritual growth. It was established with the guidance of HH Gopal Krishna Goswami Maharaj and opened its doors to the public on January 31, 2007, with a grand welcome by over 8,000 devotees. To honor the temple, the Mayor of Ghaziabad named the temple area “Hare Krishna Square” and the road leading to it “Hare Krishna Marg.

The ISKCON Wavecity Temple is still under construction, and your heartfelt contribution can help us bring this sacred place to life sooner.

Why Donate to ISKCON Wavecity? 

Your donation supports seva, feeds the needy, and helps build a spiritual legacy. 

  • Every Donation Is Seva to Lord Krishna.
    - When you give, you are not just helping a cause you are offering service (seva) directly to Krishna. 
  • Feed the Needy, Serve with Compassion
    - Your support helps us serve daily prasadam (meal donation) to hungry children, families, and the underprivileged.

  • Support Temple Construction (Mandir Nirman)
    - Help build a grand temple at ISKCON Wavecity—a spiritual home for thousands of devotees and future generations.
  • Eligible for 80G Tax Benefits
    - Every donation is tax-deductible under Section 80G, offering you both spiritual and financial benefit.
  • Be Part of a Lasting Legacy
    - Your contribution becomes part of a larger mission to serve humanity and spread Krishna consciousness.
