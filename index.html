Sri Sri Radha Mohan Madan Temple

ISKCON Wavecity Temple, also known as Sri Sri Radha Madan Mohan Mandir, is a peaceful place dedicated to Vedic learning & spiritual growth. It was established with the guidance of HH Gopal Krishna Goswami Maharaj and opened its doors to the public on January 31, 2007, with a grand welcome by over 8,000 devotees. To honor the temple, the Mayor of Ghaziabad named the temple area “Hare Krishna Square” and the road leading to it “Hare Krishna Marg.

The ISKCON Wavecity Temple is still under construction, and your heartfelt contribution can help us bring this sacred place to life sooner.
